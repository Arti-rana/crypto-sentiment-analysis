# Project Summary

This project explores the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment and trader performance.

## Methodology
Data was cleaned and preprocessed, and sentiment labels were aligned with trading data. Key metrics such as PnL and trade size were analyzed.

## Insights
Results show that sentiment influences volatility but does not directly determine profitability. Extreme market conditions lead to higher risk.

## Strategy
Traders should focus on risk management rather than relying solely on market sentiment.
